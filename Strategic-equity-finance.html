Fund Level Finance

We are specialists in arranging, structuring and advising on fund financing solutions for private equity & private credit funds (including funds of funds). Examples include:

  • - subscription financing, secured on capital call rights;
  • - finance against fund interests (NAV based) (pre & post IPO) for general or opportunistic leverage;
  • - acquisition of tail end portfolios and to finance return of capital to investors; and
  • - finance for non-standard funds or segregated accounts, containing nonstandard or complex assets.

Our experience

We are connected with over 70 lending desks spread across over 30 investment banks, alongside a growing range of non bank lenders including specialist private debt funds, pension funds, insurance companies and family offices.

Our strong relationships with lenders active in this space means we are ideally placed to most effectively match the requirements of our clients with lender offerings.

We run highly competitive processes, across structures, pricing terms and covenants, bringing deep commercial knowledge of the space.
