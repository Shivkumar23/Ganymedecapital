Debt Advisory

Specialist debt advisors operating across private markets

We advise private markets asset managers and private companies on their debt and wider capital needs, both at fund level and asset level.

Our practice areas split into three - Fund Finance, Portfolio Company Finance and Strategic Equity Finance.

In Fund Finance, from being a relatively small and niche offering until recently, we now count nearly 200 lenders globally active in offering everything from subscription line finance to NAV based finance on concentrated pools of private assets.

In Portfolio Company and Strategic Equity finance, we actively follow the growing range of non-bank lenders adding to the choice available.

With our knowledge of the lenders’ businesses, and our experience at running large private market lending businesses inside multiple investment banks, we can help you optimise the spread, structure and terms of your financing needs, and project manage the process from beginning to end.

Our principals bring over 70 years of senior debt experience.

As lenders, we have between us headed the fund finance business at both Deutsche Bank and UBS Investment Bank, as well as heading principal finance areas of Barclays and strategic equity divisions of HSBC. We also bring 14 years of specialist debt advisory experience.

In the last two years, we have now advised on and arranged over $2bn of finance across private equity, private debt, real estate and infrastructure. We have represented multiple $100bn+ managers all the way to new managers on their first fund, and represented many large independently owned private businesses.
